Downloading Logisim 3.8.0

Learn how to download Logisim.

Logisim's download packages are hosted on Github, which does not automatically detect your computer's specifications to select the right package for you. You will have to select the correct package to download manually.
  1. Determine your operating system and CPU type.
    Find your system specifications in your computer's About page in settings.
  2. Click the corresponding link to your computer's specifications in Logisim 3.8.0 Download Links.
    The download will start in your web browser.
  3. Click on the download once it is complete and follow the installation process of your computer.
Congratulations! You have successfully installed Logisim.
Confirm that Logisim has been successfully installed by making sure that Logisim shows up in your application finder and launches successfully.
